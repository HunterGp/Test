---Controls---
Rotate Cube: W, A, S, D, Q, E or Hold Right Mouse Button and Drag
Rotate Front Side: NUM 5, K
Rotate Top Side: NUM 8, I
Rotate Bottom Side: NUM 2, ","
Rotate Right Side: NUM 6, L
Rotate Left Side: NUM 4, J
Rotate Side in opposite direction: Hold Shift + Side you want to turn
